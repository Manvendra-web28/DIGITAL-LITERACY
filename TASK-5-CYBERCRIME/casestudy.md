Phishing Case Study 

Background:
A financial services company was targeted by a phishing email pretending to be from IT, asking employees to reset their passwords.

Attack:

Email contained a fake login link.
Several employees entered credentials, giving attackers access to internal systems.
Hackers sent more phishing emails and attempted fraud.

Impact:

Compromised accounts
Exposure of sensitive data
Financial losses and reputational damage

Response:

Password resets and MFA implementation
Employee training on phishing awareness
Removal of phishing emails

Lessons Learned:

Verify email sources and URLs
Enable multi-factor authentication
Report suspicious emails immediately
Conduct regular security training
