Phishing Prevention Checklist

Email Awareness

 Verify the sender’s email address before clicking links
 Look for spelling mistakes, unusual language, or urgent requests
 Do not open attachments from unknown sources

Link & Credential Safety

 Hover over links to check the actual URL before clicking
 Never enter passwords on suspicious or unverified websites
 Use strong, unique passwords for each account

Account Security

 Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) wherever possible
 Regularly update passwords and security questions
 Monitor accounts for unusual activity

Technical Measures

 Use updated antivirus and anti-phishing software
 Keep operating systems and applications updated
 Implement email filters to block suspicious messages

Reporting & Training

 Report phishing emails to IT or the appropriate authority
 Conduct regular security awareness training for all employees
 Review and update company policies on phishing and data security
